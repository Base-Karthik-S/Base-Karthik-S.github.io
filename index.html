Adversarial ML · MSc Group Project

Robust Keystroke Classification under Adversarial Conditions

Multi-model evaluation framework for acoustic side-channel keystroke inference with adversarial robustness testing and a blockchain-backed audit trail.

flip for details

Highlights

  • Fair comparison of SVM, Random Forest, LSTM, LogMel CNN and CoAtNet models on identical data
  • Structure-aware attacks: FGSM, BIM, PGD for differentiable models; DFT perturbations for the rest
  • Key finding: highest clean accuracy ≠ most secure — LSTM won on robustness (91.33% score, 0.48% attack success rate)
  • Winning model hashed and committed to an Ethereum (Sepolia) ledger for a tamper-evident audit trail
